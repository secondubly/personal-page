about

Hello! I’m Tarik and this is my little corner of the internet. I’ve got a blog, projects, and eventually a bunch of other things. I am a second-generation Caribbean software engineer with experience in both frontend, backend, and fullstack work. I’ve got an unhealthy obsession with videogames, anime/manga, mystery novels, learning languages (currently working on both Japanese and Spanish), and sometimes writing.

This website contains (and never will contain) no AI, ads, tracking, no affiliate links or sponsorship of any kind, this place is where I come to unload and unwind and to be unapologetically myself.
